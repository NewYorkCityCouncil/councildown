# 2013-2023 Council District Shapefile

2013-2023 Council District Shapefile

## Usage

``` r
nycc_cd_13
```

## Format

\## \`nycc_cd_13\` An sf collection with 51 rows and 7 columns w/
multiploygon geography:

- coun_dist:

  Council District

- Shape_Leng, Shape_Area:

  Length and Area of Council Dsitrict

- lab_coord:

  Coordinates in Matrix Form

- lab_x lab_y:

  Longitude and Latitude

- geography:

  Multipolygon of Council District
